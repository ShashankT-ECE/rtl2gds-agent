Design: 8-bit ALU
Inputs: A (8-bit), B (8-bit), opcode (3-bit)
Output: Y (8-bit), zero_flag (1-bit)
Operations:
  000 - ADD: Y = A + B
  001 - SUB: Y = A - B
  010 - AND: Y = A & B
  011 - OR:  Y = A | B
  100 - XOR: Y = A ^ B
  101 - NOT: Y = ~A
  110 - SHL: Y = A << 1
  111 - SHR: Y = A >> 1
zero_flag: assertes to 1 when Y == 0
Combinational design. No clock required.
