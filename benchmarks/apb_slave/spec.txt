Design: APB Slave with 4 registers
Module name: apb_slave
Inputs: PCLK, PRESETn, PSEL, PENABLE, PWRITE, PADDR (8-bit), PWDATA (32-bit)
Outputs: PRDATA (32-bit), PREADY (1-bit), PSLVERR (1-bit)
Behavior:
  Implements APB protocol (AMBA 2.0)
  4 internal 32-bit registers at addresses 0x00 0x04 0x08 0x0C
  Write: PSEL=1 PENABLE=1 PWRITE=1 - write PWDATA to PADDR register
  Read: PSEL=1 PENABLE=1 PWRITE=0 - output register at PADDR on PRDATA
  PREADY always 1 (zero wait states)
  PSLVERR=1 if address out of range
  PRESETn is active-low synchronous reset
